# about me

welcome. a quick fun fact about me is that i grew up across four continents and picked up english, portuguese, spanish, and enough arabic to get by.

these days i split my time between two things that turn out to be the same skill pointed at different problems: **making things fly**, and **figuring out what/where people are hiding.**

## 🚀 engineering

rockets, flight dynamics, and the math.

* 🛰️ **[fennec i–ii](https://github.com/aristox2/fennec-mission)** (2024–2025) — two-vehicle sounding rocket program. fennec i scrubbed on the pad after payload avionics caught an oxidizer pressure anomaly; fennec ii flew to 35,050 ft agl and its two independent altimeters disagreed on peak velocity (mach 1.49 vs 1.85). built a raspberry pi + xbee ground station, bench-tested extensively, hardware issue prevented live reception on both attempts. current work: ukf reconciling the two altimeters against payload imu integration. *c#, unity, python.*
* 🚀 **[osha v](https://github.com/aristox2/osha-v-damp-analysis)** (2024) — transonic (mach 1.1+), 248-step trajectory resolution; held a 1.2–3.6 caliber static margin — stable without over-stabilizing. *python, numpy, fusion 360.*
* ✈️ **er/a-1 "songbird"** — isr hypersonic concept on a turbine-based combined-cycle (tbcc) architecture; analyzed ir/uv exhaust signatures. *openvsp.*
* 🔥 **oberth** (2024) — hybrid-propulsion rocket; sized combustion stability, max-q structural tolerances, and isp. *fusion 360.*

also a lot of time on sensor fusion (ukf over 9-dof imu + baro) and 4-dof flight simulators.

## 🔍 intelligence

graphs, graphs, and more graphs!.

* 🕸️ **[cardinal](https://github.com/aristox2/ira_analysis)** — modeled 2.9m russian bot tweets with eigenvector centrality; network position distinguishes structural roles in the coordinated network — amplifier-leaning accounts sit more centrally in the hashtag co-occurrence graph than originator-leaning accounts.
* 🔎 **[bido](https://github.com/aristox2/bido)** — sanctions-evasion detection engine; c++ graph core, multilingual entity extraction (python/spacy). source not public.
* 🌐 **melusine** — closed research on state tolerance of non-state financing networks
